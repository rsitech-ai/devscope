# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We commit to a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone, regardless of
background, identity, experience, education, appearance, or ability.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and constructive.
- Assume good intent while addressing the actual technical or community impact.
- Accept correction, protect private information, and give credit.
- Keep discussion relevant to the project and its users.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized attention, deliberate
intimidation, doxxing, sustained disruption, and retaliation are not tolerated.

## Reporting and enforcement

For ordinary moderation, contact the maintainers through a repository discussion
or issue. For a private report, email `info@rsitech.ai` with the subject
`DevScope conduct report`, or use GitHub's private vulnerability-reporting flow
described in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) and prefix the report title with
`Conduct report`. Do not include more personal data than necessary.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently
restrict participation when behavior is unsafe or persistently disruptive.
Reports will be handled as privately as the investigation permits. This policy
applies in project spaces and when someone publicly represents the project.
